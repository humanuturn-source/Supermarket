Customer Return Policy:

- Food items cannot be returned or exchanged due to health and safety regulations.
- Electronics items can be exchanged within 30 days with a 5% restocking fee, provided they are in original packaging and unused.
- Medicines cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.
- All other items can be returned within 14 days for a full refund with proof of purchase.
- Returns require a valid receipt and items must be in resalable condition.

For questions, contact customer service.
